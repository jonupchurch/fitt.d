Senior Frontend Engineer
Acme Cloud — Remote

We're looking for a Senior Frontend Engineer to help modernize our
customer-facing dashboard. You'll work closely with design and backend
teams to ship fast, accessible, well-tested interfaces.

Responsibilities
- Build and maintain React/TypeScript applications used by thousands of
  customers daily.
- Own frontend architecture decisions, including state management and
  component design.
- Partner with backend engineers on API design.
- Mentor other engineers and contribute to a strong engineering culture.

Requirements
- 4+ years of professional experience with React and TypeScript.
- Strong understanding of accessibility (WCAG) best practices.
- Experience with automated testing (unit and end-to-end).
- Familiarity with cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP, or Azure) is a plus.
- Experience with Kubernetes or container orchestration is a plus.

Nice to have
- Experience with design systems.
- Familiarity with Next.js or similar meta-frameworks.
